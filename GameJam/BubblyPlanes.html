Overview

Bubbly Planes is a fast-paced local split-screen multiplayer game where players take control of delicate yet agile paper planes, navigating through environments like a small bathroom, a regular bathroom, and a chaotic car wash. The challenge? Keep your plane in the air by managing speed and moisture levels while competing to earn the most points! Glide through soap bubbles, pop them for bonuses, and avoid hazards like bubble-shooting cannons, spinning car wash brushes, and even attacks from your opponent! With dynamic obstacles and interactive physics-based mechanics, Bubbly Planes is an exciting mix of strategy, skill, and pure airborne fun. Will you master the art of paper flight and claim victory? Let the bubbly battle begin!

Role: Gameplay/Shader developer
Type: Game Jam
Duration: 48 hours
Technologies: C++, Unreal Engine 5, Shaders

Responsibilities

  • Created a comic-book-style post-processing pipeline using dithering, luminance calculations, and outline rendering.
  • Implemented screen-space outline effects based on G-Buffer normal and depth textures.
  • Developed gameplay mechanics involving interactive bubbles that apply various boosters and penalties.

Technical Implementation

  • Custom post-process materials utilizing scene texture sampling.
  • Luminance-based thresholding for stylized shading.
  • Split-screen rendering considerations and scene texture handling.

What I Learned

Gained experience handling scene textures and multi-pass post-processing workflows. Learned how split-screen rendering can introduce additional complexity in screen-space effects.

What I Learned

How split screen can be sometimes complicated to implement and user scene texture in order to achieve this comic book effect
