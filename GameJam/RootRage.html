A stylized project featuring a real-time ASCII post-processing effect combined with gameplay and AI systems.

Overview

In 2138, an AI named, G37PT21, which developed self-awareness, produced a virus Mastering every synthetic tissue... Virus began immediately, to break through the world's information systems and grow. The only way to get rid of the plague is to get to the Root... In order to do this, humanity sends synthetic androids. We called this plague... ROOTRAGE

Role: Gameplay/Shader developer
Type: Game Jam
Duration: 48 hours
Technologies: C#, Unity Built-in, Shaders

Responsibilities

  • Developed a post-processing shader that converts rendered pixels into ASCII characters in real time.
  • Implemented core gameplay mechanics.
  • Designed and programmed an enemy AI system that dynamically chases the player.

Technical Implementation

  • Per-pixel luminance analysis to map brightness values to ASCII character sets.
  • Texture-based character atlas sampling.
  • Custom post-process material integrated into the rendering pipeline.

What I Learned

Developed a deeper understanding of shader-based screen-space manipulation and techniques for mapping pixel intensity values to discrete character representations.
